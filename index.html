What I Do

I am a “generalist” in the computing field. I have a wide range of in depth experience in computing which gives me the ability to adapt to your situation and offer the best possible solution for your situation. I can evaluate your computer, network or mobile device and answer any questions you may have about their optimization and operation. Some things you may want to have me look at are:

  • Malware recovery and repair: Clean up the mess after a nasty virus or worm.
  • System Backup Setup: Keep your data from being lost in the event of a disaster. Backup your data.
  • Operating system updates and repair: Make sure your operating system is working smoothly.
  • Web filter Solutions: Keep your children safe from inappropriate content on the web.
  • Antivirus Solutions: Keep your computer secure from malware and hackers.
  • Tune-up software solutions: Optimize your PC with the best Tune-up software available.
  • Hardware Upgrades and Repair: PC slow? A RAM or Hard drive upgrade may be what you need.
  • Software troubleshooting: Need help getting software up and running?
  • Internet troubleshooting: Internet Slow? The ISP can make mistakes.
  • Network setup
  • Home entertainment network & device setup

My Experience

My experience comes from several directions. In the US Marines, I was trained on Microsoft and Cisco systems and worked on DOD networks. I was responsible for everything from PC repair to configuring Network routers and switches. On leaving the Marines, I worked as a network consultant and a network administrator with local VT companies. There I learned a wide range of systems including Sonicwall, Citrix, Linux, HP and more. I then decided to use my GI bill and, with the help of the VA, started going to college for computer science. There, I received a lot of training in computer programming and web programming.
